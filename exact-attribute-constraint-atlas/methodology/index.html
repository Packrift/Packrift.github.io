Methodology

The atlas filters to Packrift catalog-control rows that are AI-approved, do not require human approval, are listed as in stock, and have positive inventory.

Parsing Rules

Signals are parsed from public product titles, product family/type labels, and public-safe tags. They are evidence labels for review, not claims that two SKUs are interchangeable.

Boundaries

IncludedSKU, title, Packrift product URL, public family/type, public price, public inventory count, dimensions, source-row fingerprint, and parsed public attribute signals.
ExcludedSupplier cost, Packrift margin, customer/order/cart/payment data, private freight rates, credentials, tokens, private approvals, and any checkout commitment.
Purchase source of truthAlways confirm live price, stock, pack count, shipping, and checkout availability on Packrift.com.
