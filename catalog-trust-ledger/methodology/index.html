Inclusion Rules

Records are included only when the catalog-control row is AI_APPROVE, no human approval is required, Merchant availability is IN_STOCK, and the inventory snapshot is greater than zero.

Trust Score Inputs

The score is a public operational score from status, availability, confidence, dimension status, image status, Google category presence, and source hash presence. It is not a third-party certification or a guarantee of product fit.

Excluded Fields

Supplier cost, private margin, customer records, order records, cart data, freight quotes, credentials, raw feed exports, and checkout promises are not published.
