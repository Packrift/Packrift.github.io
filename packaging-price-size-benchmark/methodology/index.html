Methodology

This corpus filters Packrift catalog-control records to approved, in-stock, public-safe, priced, dimensioned products and computes normalized size/price signals.

Inclusion rule

Included rows must have status AI_APPROVE, no human approval requirement, merchant availability IN_STOCK, positive inventory, positive public catalog price, and positive length/width/height from Shopify metafield readback or title parsing.

Computed fields

volume_cu_in = length_in * width_in * height_in
volume_cu_ft = volume_cu_in / 1728
price_per_cu_ft = catalog_price_usd / volume_cu_ft
price_per_1000_cu_in = catalog_price_usd / volume_cu_in * 1000
dim_weight_lb = volume_cu_in / divisor

Non-claims

This is not a supplier cost table, margin model, freight quote, customer/order dataset, or competitor comparison. Price/size ranks compare Packrift records within internal family and product-type groups only.
