What this benchmark contains

Normalized unit economics

Each record includes public catalog price, cubic inches, cubic feet, price per cubic foot, price per 1,000 cubic inches, and dimensional-weight estimates.

Public evidence trail

Every SKU card links to the public Packrift product URL and carries the public source-row hash plus a release-specific benchmark hash.

Strict boundary

No supplier cost, margin, order, payment, cart, customer, private freight, or competitor-price claim is included.
